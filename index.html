Jalandhar was once ruled by King Arjan Singh. It is believed that the city came to be named Jalandhar for various reasons. Some say that it was named after a demon king named “Jalandhara.” And some say that it derives its name from the Hindi word Jalandhar which means they are inside the water referring to the area lying in between Sutlej and Beas. The entire Jalandhar was a part of the Indus valley civilization.

Famous Places

Science City

Address: Jalandhar Kapurthala Road, Punjab, India 144601

Science City Kapurthala, otherwise known as Pushpa Gujral Science City is a haven for science enthusiasts established by the Government of Punjab.

Wonderland

Address: Jalandhar - Nakodar Rd, Wadala Chowk, After, Jalandhar, Punjab 144001

Park with family-friendly rides, including a small roller coaster, plus water slides & a wave pool.

Rangla Punjab

Address: Jalandhar - Phagwara Highway, Khajurla, Grand Trunk Rd, Next to Haveli, Jalandhar, Punjab 144001

Highliting the rich culture of punjab, Rangla punjab is the best place to learn about the culture of punjab and to hang out with family.

Rainak Bazar

Rainak Bazaar also known as the wholesale market. One can always find amazing things for cheapest price.



Religious Places

Devi Talab Mandir

Address: Tanda Rd, Shiv Nagar, Industrial Area, Jalandhar, Punjab 144004

Devi Talab Mandir or Devi Talab Temple s devoted to Goddess Durga and is believed to be at least 200 years old. Thousands of pilgrims visit the temple every year and is one of the 51 Shakthi pithas in India.

Talhan Sahib Gurdwara

Address: Jalandhar Ludhiana Road Near, Jalandhar Cantt, Talhan, Punjab 144010

Shaheed Baba Nihal Singh Gurdwara commonly known as Talhan Sahib Gurdwara is a small shrine which is dedicated to Baba Nihal Singh, a divine person who helped locals in making pulleys for water wells and worked for the betterment of the society.

Gurdwara Chhevin Padshahi

Address: Basti Sheik, Jalandhar City, Punjab, India

Gurdwara Chhevin Padshahi is an important religious landmark that holds a significant cultural value. The Gurdwara Chhevin Padshahi is built at a place where Guruji address Muslim holy saint popularly known as Sheikh Darvesh.

Geeta Mandir

Dedicated to Lord Krishna and Radha, this famous temple in Jalandhar, Punjab that is located near Nikku Park. The main attraction of this temple is the idol of the Radha Krishna that attracts a large number of devotees every year who come to pay their respects.

Dera Baba Murad Shah Ji

Address: Baba Murad Shah Ji Road, Amardas Colony, Nakodar, Punjab 144040

Dera Baba Murad Shah Ji is a Sufiyana Darbar located in Nakodar, Jalandhar Punjab, India. The Darbar is a symbol of love and peace where people from all different castes and religions come and pay their respects at this Darbar.

St. Mary's Cathedral

Address: 59, The Mall Rd, Jalandhar Cantt, Jalandhar, Punjab 144005

St. Mary's Cathedral in Jalandhar, Punjab, India, is the seat of the bishop of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Jalandhar. The cathedral is located in the Jalandhar Cantonment. The old church at the same site was dedicated to St. Patrick



Famous Personalities

Harbhajan Singh

Harbhajan Singh Plaha, commonly known as Harbhajan or simply Bhajji or Bhajju Pa, is an Indian cricketer, who has played all formats of cricket for India. specialist spin bowler, he has the second-highest number of Test wickets by an off spinner

Rannvijay Singha

Rannvijay Singh Singha is an Indian actor, television personality and VJ. Rannvijay is known for his appearance in MTV Roadies, where he won the show.He has been part of the show from 2004 to 2019 and continues to do so

The Great Khali

Dalip Singh Rana, better known by the ring name The Great Khali, is an Indian-born American professional wrestler, promoter, model, and actor who is best known for his work in WWE.

Ranvir Shorey

Ranvir Shorey is an Indian actor and former VJ. Since making his debut in Ek Chhotisi Love Story, he has starred in a number of high-profile films such as Jism and Lakshya

Varun Sharma

Varun Sharma is an Indian film actor who has starred in commercially successful films including Fukrey, Kis Kisko Pyaar Karoon, Dilwale, Fukrey Returns, and Chhichhore.

Famous Fairs

Sodal Mela

Varun Sharma is an Indian film actor who has starred in commercially successful films including Fukrey, Kis Kisko Pyaar Karoon, Dilwale, Fukrey Returns, and Chhichhore.

Nakodar Mela

The dargah of Baba Murad Shah ji after the death of Sai Laddi Shah Ji was offered to the famous Punjabi singer, Mr. Gurdas Maan Ji who has remained as a sewadaar. Various singers perform at this powerful place and also there is a mela (fair) celebrated regularly in Memory of Baba Murad Shah Ji and Sai Ghulam (Laddi) Shah Ji

Shaheedi Jor mela

Shaheedi Jor mela, which is held annually in the memory of Shaheed Baba Nihal Singh. The mela is a big attraction in Jalandhar. An annual Mela is held to mark the death anniversary ("Barsi") of the Shaheed Baba Nihal Singh.
